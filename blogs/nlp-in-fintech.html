NLP in FinTech

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is transforming how financial institutions process unstructured data. At Credit Saison, we leverage NLP to automate complex risk assessment workflows that were previously manual and error-prone.

The Challenge

Financial statements, bank records, and legal documents come in varied formats. Extracting meaningful entities like "Total Assets", "Net Income", or "Loan Covenants" from these documents at scale requires more than just regex—it requires a semantic understanding of the text.

Our Solution: Custom NER Models

We implemented a pipeline using Python and spaCy to build custom Named Entity Recognition (NER) models. By training these models on our industry-specific datasets, we've achieved a 98% accuracy rate in automated entity extraction.

Scalability and Impact

The impact of this automation has been profound. We've increased our document processing throughput by 3x, allowing our credit analysts to focus on high-level decision-making rather than data entry. Our AI/ML models now process thousands of documents daily, feeding directly into our high-throughput validation engines.

As we continue to refine our models, we're exploring transformers and large language models (LLMs) to further enhance our zero-shot classification capabilities for new document types.
